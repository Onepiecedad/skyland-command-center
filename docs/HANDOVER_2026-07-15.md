## Sekvensmotorn (GHL-ersättningens kärna) — arkitektur

Native "workflow-motor". Ersätter GHL:s workflow-byggare.

**Tabeller** (migrations i `database/migrations/`, applicerade i Supabase):
- `sequences` — namngiven automation. trigger_type (manual/contact_created/opportunity_created/
  stage_changed/booking_created/booking_cancelled/booking_no_show/tag_added/reply_received),
  trigger_config, exit_on (jsonb-array), status (draft/active/paused). `ticket40_sequences.sql`
- `sequence_steps` — ordnade steg. type: send_email/send_sms/wait/wait_until/branch/move_stage/
  add_tag/remove_tag/create_task/webhook/exit. config jsonb. (`ticket40` + `ticket46_wait_until.sql`)
- `sequence_enrollments` — en kontakt i en sekvens. status, current_position, next_run_at, context (jsonb).
  Unik-aktiv-spärr på (sequence_id, contact_id) where active.
- `sequence_step_runs` — audit per kört steg.
- `bookings` — Cal.com-spegel (`ticket45_bookings.sql`).

**Kod** (`backend/src/`):
- `services/sequenceRunner.ts` — tick-runner + alla step-executors. `startSequenceRunner()` i server.ts,
  gatad av `SEQUENCE_RUNNER_ENABLED`. send_email/send_sms gatas dessutom av `OUTBOUND_ENABLED`.
- `services/sequenceEvents.ts` — enrollContact, fireTrigger, fireExit + onStageChanged/onReplyReceived/
  onBookingCreated/onBookingCancelled/onBookingNoShow. Kontext (t.ex. booking_start) trådas in.
- `services/bookings.ts` — mirrorBooking: speglar Cal.com-bokning, matchar kontakt (attendee-mejl),
  triggar events.
- `services/sms.ts` — 46elks-adapter + E.164-normalisering (ren, enhetstestad `sms.test.ts`).
- `services/integrationHealth.ts` — probar Supabase/Resend/Cal.com/46elks/OpenRouter (SCC-37).
- `services/attribution.ts` — kontakt-tidslinje (tvärkanal) + trattexport (SCC-36).
- `services/contactMerge.ts` — merge utan dataförlust (SCC-41).
- `routes/`: `sequences.ts`, `emailInbound.ts`, `calcomWebhook.ts`, `integrations.ts`,
  `attribution.ts`, `merge.ts`. Alla registrerade i `server.ts`.
- `llm/tools.ts` — nya Alex-verktyg: `list_sequences`, `enroll_in_sequence`.
- Frontend: `frontend/src/pages/SequencesView.tsx` + "Sekvenser"-flik i `App.tsx` + api-funktioner i `api.ts`.

**Hur det funkar:** en trigger (t.ex. Cal.com-bokning, stage-flytt) enrollar kontakten i matchande
aktiva sekvenser. Runnern (var 60:e s) plockar due enrollments, kör stegen (skickar mejl/SMS via
befintliga services, hedrar wait/wait_until, grenar, taggar, flyttar stage), avancerar, avslutar.
Exit-events (svar inkommet, bokning) avslutar aktiva drips. "Aldrig tyst skip" (GHL-härledd SCC-38):
saknar kontakt mejl/telefon → synlig warn-activity, inte tyst bortfall.
